How language major student became a data scientist?
Follow my history below.

I have always been a curious human-being. Questioning everything and anything around the world. First degree in language interpretation/translation and second degree in social science. Framing story, finding core message in speech, and make it understood by audience. That's what I learned during my translation studies, and data science work has a lot in common.

After landing in professional world, I immediately fell in love with data science. It allowed me to dig in the subject and drives business decisions. I followed my third(!) degree in data analytics/science. So far, data digging never made me bored. We have tremendous problems to explore and data science is evolving all the time.

During day time, I am a data lover and metor. It's thrilling to dive into data and share findings. Helping out inviduals is rewarding. During night, I make jewerly, or turn into a dancer 🤸. Minimalism, Sustainability, Kindness, Open-Mindedness are words I try to live by. I was born in South Korea, passed through Australia, thrown away in Belgium since 2016.
